Let's Explore

Welcome to the Gulp Coast

Here among the palm trees, the cities of Tampa Bay have a beer and a taproom that will suit a craft beer afficionado of any stripe.

With over 80 breweries in the area, the Bay surpasses even some of the largest metropolitan areas across the nation when it comes to its choice of craft beers. Whether India Pale Ales, sour beers, blondes, or belgian ales are your thing, there is guaranteed to be a taproom, or several, that will make your trip worth it.

World Class

The breweries of Tampa Bay have been producing award-winning craft beers for decades. In a survey conducted by Ratebeer.com, respondents named three Tampa Bay breweries to its best in the world list.

Local favorite Cigar City Brewing led the city by coming in at #5. Cigar City's popularity transcends the Bay with popular brews such as the Jai Alai IPA, Maduro brown ale, and Guayabera citra ale being distributed across the United States. Cycle Brewing, the highest-rated brewery in St. Petersburg, cracked the top 25 at #20. Bike-themed beers such as Crank and Fixie are a welcome refreshments for those touring the beautiful murals of the St. Pete downtown. Lastly, Angry Chair Brewing, located in the up-and-coming Seminole Heights neighborhood, made it in the list at #78.

Did You Know?

Tampa Bay has the greatest concentration of craft breweries in Florida.
